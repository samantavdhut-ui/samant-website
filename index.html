My Alphonso trees show black spots on leaves. Is it anthracnose?
AI Advisor
Black spots on Alphonso during pre-monsoon are often anthracnose (Colletotrichum gloeosporioides). Recommended: Copper oxychloride 0.3% + Mancozeb 0.25% spray. Repeat in 15 days.
You
What humidity triggers powdery mildew in Konkan?
AI Advisor
Risk rises when RH stays above 70% with 22–28°C — typically Feb–March. Preventive Sulphur dust (30 kg/ha) at panicle emergence is the standard protocol.
